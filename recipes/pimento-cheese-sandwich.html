Pimento Cheese Sandwich

A pimento cheese sandwich on a nice summer evening is unbeatable.

Enjoy this recipe and make it a stable in your home!

Ingredients:

  • The correct amount of pimento cheese
  • 2 slices of white bread

Steps:

  1. Spread the pimento cheese onto 1 side of a slice of bread
  2. Sandwich the other slice of bread with the spreaded side of the other slice
  3. Congratulations and enjoy!
