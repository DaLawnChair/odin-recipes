Tea

A nice simple drink for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of tea leaves
  • 1 cup of water

Steps:

  1. Boil water
  2. Add tea leaves and boiling hot water to a bowl
  3. Wait ~10 or so minutes until the tea is at drinking temperature
  4. Pour tea through a strainer into a cup
  5. Add sugars, creamers, and/or ice if you'd like
  6. Enjoy your tea
